Privacy Policy

Data We Collect

Spendly collects the information you voluntarily provide when you create an account and record expenses. This includes your name, email address, and the expense data you enter (amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions).

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@spendly.com.
